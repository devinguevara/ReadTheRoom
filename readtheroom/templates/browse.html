Danceability

Danceability is a measure of how easily one can dance to a piece of music. Danceability is measured on a scale of 0-1, with a greater value indicating greater danceability. Databases like those created by Spotify calculate danceability by analyzing a song's volume, beat strength, tempo, and beat strength.

Energy

A song's energy is also measured on a scale of 0-1 and is typically a function of a song's intensity and loudness. The louder and faster a song is, the more energy it is considered to have.

Genre

ELECTRONIC AND DANCE

Music that is typically for social dancing, particularly in clubs. This genre is characterized by repetitive beats and high energy.

FOLK

Music that was traditionally passed down orally though generations or small social groups.

POP

Popular mainstream music that can encompass a variety of music genres. Music of this genre is typically catchy and great for karaokes.

R&B

Rhythm and Blues music initially referred to a combination of jazz and blues that became popularized in the '40s and was developed by Black artists. Today, this genre describes music with strong gospel, rock, jazz, and folk influences.

ROCK & ROLL

Music that is typically characterized by strong vocals, beats, and rhythms. Rock songs are generally very danceable and have high energies.

WORLD

Music from around the world, or non-Western music.

HIP HOP

Hip Hop music is characterized by strong, rhythmic beats and rhyming lyrics. This genre of music originated in Black American communities in the 1970s and is now one of the most popular music genres in the United States and the world at large.

COUNTRY

Country music originated in the southern states of the United States and is characterized by the presence of stringed instruments and typically slower, narrative lyrics.

LATIN

Latin music encompasses a wide range of music from countries across Latin America, from Mexico and Peru to Cuba and Puerto Rico. Latin music is typically very high in danceability.

METAL

METAL: Music with strong beats and rhythms and a loud volume. This genre is often described as a powerful and aggressive style of music.
